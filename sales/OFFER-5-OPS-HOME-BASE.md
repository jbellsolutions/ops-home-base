## The Core Insight (Todd Brown "Big Idea")

Here's the problem nobody's solving:

**The average operations person has 4-7 apps open at all times.** ClickUp in one tab. CRM in another. Gmail in a third. Calendar in a fourth. Slack in a fifth. They spend their day alt-tabbing between apps, copy-pasting information, manually updating statuses, and trying to keep everything in sync.

They're not doing operations. They're doing **app management.**

The Ops Home Base flips this entirely:

Instead of YOU going to each app, **one AI goes to every app for you.** You stay in one place. You talk. It executes.

- "What's on my calendar tomorrow?" → It checks Google Calendar and tells you.
- "Create a task for Audrey to document all CRM pipelines, due Wednesday, high priority" → It creates it in ClickUp, assigns it, sets the date, sets the priority.
- "Show me my unread emails from HighLevel" → It searches Gmail and shows you.
- "Move the Johnson deal to 'Proposal Sent' in the CRM" → It updates Go High Level.

**You don't learn the apps. You don't click through menus. You don't copy-paste between tabs. You just say what you need, and the AI does the rest.**

This isn't automation (if X then Y). This is a **conversational operations layer** — an AI that understands your business context, has direct connections to your tools, and executes in plain English.
